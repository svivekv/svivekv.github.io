Privacy Issues in Sharing Platforms

Sonata Software Ltd., Bangalore, IN
Principal Investigator

₹ 10,500,000

3 years (Feb 2018 - Feb 2021)


The sharing platforms such as Uber, Airbnb, Expedia etc., have radically altered the landscape of the transport, travel and leisure economy. This necessitates collection of users’ and service providers’ personal information and the usage statistics. Recent reports and studies on privacy issues arising in the use of sharing platforms point out glaring instances of misuse of personal information. The objective of this project is to investigate and broaden the applicability of encryption technology to address privacy issues in the sharing platforms. In particular, we want to investigate the use of Homomorphic Encryption technology in the context of enhancing privacy in taxi service applications.
